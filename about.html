About Us: Ruwan Auto Service Center

Ruwan Auto Service Center has been providing reliable and affordable vehicle servicing for all your needs since January 2008. We are a one-stop shop for servicing bikes, tricycles, cars, vans, lorries, and more! Our team utilizes modern equipment and top-quality materials to ensure your vehicle receives the best possible care. Conveniently located in Gorakadeniya Domain of Gampaha District, Ruwan Auto is proud to serve the community seven days a week.

More than just a service station: At Ruwan Auto, we understand that your vehicle is an important part of your life. That's why we go the extra mile to provide exceptional customer service. Our friendly and knowledgeable technicians will take the time to explain any recommended repairs and answer your questions in a clear and concise manner. We are committed to building trust and long-term relationships with our customers.

Investing in your peace of mind: Regular maintenance is essential for keeping your vehicle running smoothly and safely. By choosing Ruwan Auto, you can be confident that your car, truck, or bike is in good hands. We offer a variety of services to meet your individual needs, from routine oil changes and tune-ups to major repairs. We also stay up-to-date on the latest automotive technologies to ensure we can service any vehicle, regardless of make or model.
